Modifying Graphic Elements

To resize a circle or ellipse, or to resize or reshape a rectangle:

  1. Select the element.
  1. Move the mouse to the circumference of the circle or ellipse, or to a side or corner of the rectangle. When you are in position, the pointer changes to a crosshair shape (+).
  1. Click-and-drag to reshape the graphic element as required, then release the mouse.

To reshape a line, line series, polygon, or arc:

  1. Click on an endpoint of the line or arc. In a line series or polygon, click on any endpoint or corner.
  1. While holding the mouse button down, drag the mouse to reshape the graphic element.
  1. When the element appears as you need it, release the mouse.
