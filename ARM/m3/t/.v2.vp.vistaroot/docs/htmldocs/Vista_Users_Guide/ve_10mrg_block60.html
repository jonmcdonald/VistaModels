Drawing in the Symbol Editor

The following sections describe how to use the Symbol Editor tools to draw the geometric representation that you need for a component symbol. After you have drawn a shape or line using one of the tools, you can move and resize it.

To activate the grid in the Symbol Editor window to aid in drawing a symbol, choose View > Show Grid or press the F6 key.

Drawing a Circle or Ellipse

To draw a circle or ellipse:

  1. Click the Circle icon: .
  1. Click where you need to position the center of the circle/ellipse.

As you move the mouse away from the center, the radius of the circle increases or, alternatively, the size and shape of the ellipse change.

  1. Click to fix the size of the shape.
Drawing a Rectangle

To draw a rectangle:

  1. Click the Rectangle icon: .
  1. Click where you want to place one corner of the rectangle.
  1. Click where you want to place the diagonally opposite corner of the rectangle.
Drawing Straight Lines

To draw a straight line or a series of connected line segments:

  1. Click the Line icon: .
  1. Click where you need the line to start.
  1. Move the mouse to dictate the direction of the line.
  1. Click once wherever you need a corner.
  1. Double-click to terminate a line, or the last of a series of connected lines.
Drawing a Polygon

To draw a closed polygon:

  1. Click the Line icon: .
  1. Click where you need the first edge of the polygon to start.
  1. Move the mouse to dictate the direction of the line.
  1. Click once wherever you need to end the current line segment and begin the next line segment.
  1. Continue extending line segments and their adjacent angles until you return to the starting point of the polygon.
  1. Double-click to complete the polygon.
Drawing an Arc

To draw an arc of a circle:

  1. Click the Arc icon: .
  1. Click where you need to place one endpoint of the arc.
  1. Click where you need to place the other endpoint of the arc.
  1. Move your mouse to create the angle of the arc.
  1. Click to fix the angle.
