Improving Block Diagram Appearance

Sometimes, block diagrams become too cluttered, and channel routing becomes so complex that it is very difficult to clearly see the inter-connectivity between components.

To this end, Vista provides several commands that automatically rearrange components, and reroute channels to make the block diagram look more organized and simpler to comprehend.

You can access these commands through the Tools > Place & Route submenu. The Reroute Only command, for example, reroutes channels only. Other commands also affect component position and size.

These Place & Route commands can also be performed on a selected group of elements.
