Analysis Sampling Interval

Analysis results are the average value computed for each Sampling Interval constituting together the complete simulation time. Timing interval size can be decreased by clicking the “down” arrow (to divide by 10) to obtain more intervals (x10), and hence get more detailed analysis results. The size can also be increased by clicking the “up” arrow (to multiply by 10) to get less intervals and hence get less details. In addition, the Sampling Interval field is editable, enabling you to specify your user specific timing intervals.
