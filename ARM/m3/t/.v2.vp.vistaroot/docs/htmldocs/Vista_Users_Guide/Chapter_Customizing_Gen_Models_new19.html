Generic Processors (Cores)

The Generic Processor models are Fast ISS (Instruction-Set-Simulator) Models for a number of processor families (ARM, PPC, MPC). These ISS Models can be integrated into any Vista SystemC/TLM 2.0 platform and are suitable for:

  • Validation of the platform.
  • Performance analysis of the platform.
  • Software debugging and analysis (Virtual Prototype).

  Note

The Generic processor Models are based on JIT (Just In Time) compilation.
 

This mechanism ensures very high performance for models and is used by most Fast ISS providers in the market today.

The processor models are based on QEMU public domain sources (GNU LGPL) and are provided “as-is” with no vendor certification.
