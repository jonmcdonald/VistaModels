Rotating Pins and Pin Names

You can rotate one or more selected pins and their names together, or you can rotate only the names.

  1. To rotate the pin and its name, select the pin. To rotate only the name, select the name.
  1. Right-click and choose Rotate, or click the Rotate icon: .

Each click on the icon rotates the element 90° counterclockwise.

When multiple pins are selected, the axis of rotation is the center point of a rectangle defined by the outermost part of the pins to rotate.
