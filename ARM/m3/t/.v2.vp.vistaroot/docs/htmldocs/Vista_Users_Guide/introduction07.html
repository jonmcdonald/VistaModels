Modeling

Vista's GUI-based modeling module offers a powerful, high-level platform that enables engineers and system architects to efficiently and deterministically create advanced TLM models used for HW/SW validation, as well as performance and power analysis (and the trade-offs between them). As a result, Vista eliminates costly physical product cycles.

Vista model-builder models are built on two layers:

  • “PV” (Programmers' view) with pure functional behavior layer
  • “T” Timing Layer that captures the Timing and Power behavior.

These two layers are completely separate, allowing a clear distinction between functionality and implementation. The layered modeling approach enables the use of a single model for pure functional simulation (e.g. for running SW) or for comprehensive timing simulation with power behavior.

The Timing layer makes use of unique technology which introduces (via a GUI) complex timing characteristics from a small number of “Timing Policies” in a top-down flow.

See "Creating TLM Models" for detailed modeling procedures.
